What Are The Eligibility Criteria For The SLNFinserve DSA Registration Process?

SLNloans Distribution Services Private Limited (SLNloans), a registered DSA of various banks, offers a unique proposition to become a personal loan DSA in India. The Personal Loan DSA Eligibility criteria are as follows:

SLNloans Loan DSA Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age: 23-58 years old
  • Minimum Qualification: 12th pass
  • Minimum Work Experience: 1 year of experience in sales and marketing
  • Location: Pan-India
  • Good communication skills

If you meet the above eligibility criteria, you can register as a SLNloans Personal Loan DSA and start earning attractive commissions on successful loan disbursals. Join SLNloans as a Loan DSA now!

How to Become a DSA Partner with SLNFinserve?

Are you looking to leverage your network and earn attractive commissions by referring leads for loans and credit cards? Join SLNFinserve as a DSA Partner and embark on a rewarding journey with us.

  • Step 1: Fill in the online registration form with your personal details and contact information.
  • Step 2: Submit the necessary documents such as PAN Card, Aadhar Card, and educational certificates.
  • Step 3: Complete the training program provided by SLNloans to understand their products and processes.
  • Step 4: Once you clear the training, you will receive your DSA ID and access to the SLNloans portal.
  • Step 5: Start referring customers and earning commissions on successful loan disbursals.

Join SLNFinerse as a DSA Partner today and embark on a rewarding career in the financial services industry.
